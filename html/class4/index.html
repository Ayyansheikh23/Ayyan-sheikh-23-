Today's Topic Is Fruits

Eating fruits is very beneficial for health. They contain a good amount of vitamins, minerals, and fibers. Fruits provide energy to the body and increase the power to fight against diseases. Eating fruits daily improves digestion and makes the skin healthy and glowing. That is why we should definitely include fruits in our daily life.

Eating fruit causes weakness. Fruits are good for health.

  • Apple
    • Apple is a very healthy fruit. It is full of vitamins and fiber. Eating an apple every day keeps us strong and prevents many diseases. It is good for our heart, skin, and digestion. That is why people say, “An apple a day keeps the doctor away.”
    • Advantages
      1. Healthy heart
      2. Rich in Fiber
      3. Boosts Immunity
      4. Good for Skin
      5. Controls Weight
  • Banana
    • Banana is a very healthy and tasty fruit. It gives us quick energy and is rich in potassium. Eating bananas helps in digestion, keeps our heart strong, and makes our bones healthy. It is a complete fruit that keeps us active and happy
    • Advantages
      1. Instant Energy
      2. Good for Digestion
      3. Rich in Potassium
      4. Good for Bones
      5. Improves Mood
  • Mango
    • Mango is called the king of fruits. It is very sweet and full of vitamins. Eating mango makes us strong, improves our eyesight, and keeps our skin fresh. It also gives us energy and protects us from many diseases.
    • Advantages
      1. Rich in Vitamins
      2. Boosts Energy
      3. Improves Digestion
      4. Healthy Heart
      5. Healthy Heart
  • Grapes
    • Grapes are small, sweet, and juicy fruits. They are full of vitamins and antioxidants. Eating grapes keeps our blood clean, makes our heart healthy, and gives us quick energy. They also keep the body fresh and hydrated.
    • Advantages
      1. Good for Blood
      2. Rich in Antioxidants
      3. Good for Eyes
      4. Keeps Body Hydrated
      5. Boosts Energy
